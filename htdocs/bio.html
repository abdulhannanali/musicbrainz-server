      Robert Kaye (homepage)

Robert is a die hard MP3 fan dedicated to improving the digital audio experience. After studying Computer Engineering at Cal Poly he joined Xing Technology where he was part of the MP3 team and served as the development manager for the AudioCatalyst project. While at EMusic, Robert has been working on the FreeAmp and Obsequeiuem projects, besides working on MusicBrainz. Robert is now working for Bitzi who is now also a sponsor of MusicBrainz.

Robert is a complete OpenSource, Linux, Perl, Honda S2000 and Burning Man nut. Generally considered to be an all around troublemaker, its probably best to keep a safe distance from Robert at all times.

 
      Johan Pouwelse

Johan the is owner of the MP3.nl web site that is home to an extensive music database. With extensive UNIX experience, client/server programming and compression technology he developed several programs to use MP3 technology in new ways. His current project is adding MTV like Info Facts and Say What functionality to MP3 files.

A firm believer in the GNU Public License, he publishes the source code of all his work. Currently he is working on his Ph.D. at the Technical University of Delft and applying audio compression in his research. Obtained a Cum Laude masters degree after only two years of study at the same university.

We used to attempt to document the database structure of the MusicBrainz Server, but we implement features at a rapid pace and the documentation never seems to keep up with our changes. In the future we hope to change that, but for now we invite you to take a look at our CreateTables.sql script in CVS for a definitive guide to the database structure of MusicBrainz. This sql script defines the database structure currently in use on the main server and is religously kept up to date.

The other scripts in the mb_server/admin/sql directory give more details about index, foreign key, and serial number management workings of the MusicBrainz Server. Please be advised that you'll need to understand the fundamentals of SQL before these scripts will make sense to you.

<& /comp/section, title=>"Future database structure" &>

We also used to attempt to document our future plans for the database. But, as it is typical in software engineering, our implementations never matched our speculations about how we thought we'd do things in the future.

In order to get a gimpse of what we are working on the for the future, take a look a our MusicBrainz Development Wiki page which describes all sorts of work being done with MusicBrainz right now.

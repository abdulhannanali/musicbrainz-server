Usage instructions:

Move the focus either to the dropdown list or the input field on the right side. Then enter a substring of the Instruments/Vocal attribute you like to find.

Advanced matching:

If you enter multiple words, each word is matched to the subsequent word of the instrument/vocal attribute. If you enter sl g, the part sl is matched against Slide and g against Guitar, which is the only instrument matching these two word patterns.

Multiple results:

If there are multiple results, you can either use Advanced matching, or step through the results in many ways.
  • If you clicked on the dropdown list, use ARROW_LEFT and ARROW_RIGHT
  • If you clicked on the search field, use ARROW_UP and ARROW_DOWN
  • You can click the buttons « and »

Special keys:

All these fields accept the following special keys:
  • BACKSPACE or DELETE to delete the last character, one at a time
  • ESC reset the search string

Examples (for Instruments):

  • Basson bass
  • Bass Clarinet ba c
  • Alto Clarinet o c
  • Electric Bass Guitar e b g or e ba
  • Electric Guitar ele g
  • Saxophone sax
  • ...

The Companies and People who have/are supporting MusicBrainz, in alphabetical order:

    Bitzi Corporation employs Robert Kaye and allows him to spend up to half his time working on MusicBrainz.
 
    Billy Grady at the Bladerunner Day Spa is Robert's hairdresser who has created all of Robert's wicked hair cuts. Most recently he contributed his time and energy to create the P2P Hair cut.
 
    EMusic.com, Robert's previous employer who also allows Robert to work on MusicBrainz while working at EMusic. EMusic currently provides the server space and bandwidth for MusicBrainz.
 
    O'Reilly and Associates was the first company to make a cash contribution to MusicBrainz. The money from this donation will be used to promotion purposes when MusicBrainz reaches 1.0.
 
    Relatable LLC has made their TRM technology available for use in the MusicBrainz client library. The TRM technology is a core aspect of the MusicBrainz system.

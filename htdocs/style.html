The following style guidelines outline how the data in MusicBrainz should be formatted and organized. If you're going to participate in the moderation process, please take a moment to review these guidelines.

The Style Guidelines are a work in progress. Most of the current work on the guidelines is being done on the Wiki, and can be viewed on the StyleGuideline Wiki page. More information related to style guidelines can also be found on the Moderation FAQ Wiki page.

In case you want to refer other MusicBrainz users to these guidelines, each one has a small link (∗) before it, which you can "bookmark" and give to the other person.  For example, in some browsers you would right-click on the appropriate link and choose "Copy shortcut".

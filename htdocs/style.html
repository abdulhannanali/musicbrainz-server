The following style guidelines outline how the data in MusicBrainz should be formatted and organized. If you would like to participate in the moderation process, please take a moment to review these guidelines. Please take a look at the MusicBrainzForums if you require assistance.

The style guidelines are a work in progress, and will change from time to time. Most of the current work on the guidelines is being done on the StyleGuideline wiki page. More information relating to the style guidelines can also be found on the ModerationFAQ wiki page. The wiki will generally be more up to date than this page.

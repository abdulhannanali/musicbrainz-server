Questions

Please check the General FAQ, Moderation FAQ or the Tagger FAQ to see if your question has already been answered.

Mailing Lists

We have several mailing lists, including one for general discussion of MusicBrainz.  This is an excellent way to get help or ask questions about this site.

Report A Bug

Found something that doesn't work?  Please check the list of outstanding bugs and then, if your bug isn't in that list, add it in using the bug report page. 

Likewise if you've got an idea for something new or improved, please make sure it's not already on the list of outstanding RFEs before submitting a new RFE. 

E-mail Us

Support: support@musicbrainz.org

Contribute: donations@musicbrainz.org

Sponsorship enquiries: sponsors@musicbrainz.org

Other enquiries: info@musicbrainz.org

IRC

The MusicBrainz team and MusicBrainz users are often to be found in the #musicbrainz channel on IRC chat at irc.freenode.net.  Please feel free to browse the IRC log archives.
